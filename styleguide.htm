This Portfolio's Styleguide

Below you will find the basic design system I've made for this portfolio as part of the IAT 339 Project 3, as well as a citation list at the very end. The look & feel I was after would convey the theme of play and video games, but rather from a "classic", "comprehensive" angle, instead of imitating the flashy looks of modern gaming products and AAA-games' user interfaces.

Typography

ChicagoFLF

The idiosyncratic typeface from the glory days of 80s, Apple and 8-bit computing that brings associations with old arcade video games. Used for headings.


Source Code Pro

A modern monospace typeface that can be often seen in code editors. Used for paragraphs and descriptions.
